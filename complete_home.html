FAQs

1. What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day, hassle-free return policy. If your product arrives damaged or doesn't match the description, you can request a full refund or replacement within 7 days of delivery.

2. What should I do if my product is defective?

For defective or malfunctioning items, please contact our support team within 48 hours. We'll guide you through troubleshooting steps or arrange a replacement at no extra cost.

3. How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 5-10 business days within major cities and up to 7-10 days for other areas. Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email or SMS.

4. Do your smart devices come with a warranty?

Yes! All our products include a 1-year manufacturer's warranty covering mechanical or software defects. Extended warranty options are available at checkout.
